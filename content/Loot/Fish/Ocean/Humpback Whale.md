A true titan of the salt, this gentle giant is more like a floating island than a fish. It spends its Days singing songs that carry for miles through the current and breaching the surface with enough force to swamp a small boat. They don't actually have teeth; instead, they use giant plates of baleen to filter out tiny krill, meaning they’re the biggest eaters in the sea despite having some of the smallest prey.
