A real king of the sea floor, this red-plated behemoth is more of a tank than a fish. With claws as heavy as a boat's railing and armor as thick as a hull, it’s a legendary fighter that has seen more years in the deep trenches than most sailors have seen land. What’s more, these giants do not really age like us; they just keep growing and growing every time they shed their massive shell.
