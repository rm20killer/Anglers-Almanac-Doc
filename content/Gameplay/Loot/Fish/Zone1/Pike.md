Also known as the "Water Wolf," this freshwater monster is pure muscle and teeth. It lurks in the reeds like a coiled spring, ready to spring forth at anything that moves with a quick snap of its needle-filled jaws. If you ever experience a sudden, violent tug on your line in the shallows, you’ve probably hooked a Pike.
