The Catfish can be identified by its distinctive sensory barbels those whiskers that function as taste organs for water. This fish is a world-class expert at sensing the water by touch. It is basically a vacuum cleaner with a purple armor kit, thanks to its sensitive face that helps it sniff out treats buried deep in the silt where other fish are blind.
