> [!important] 
> This is a rough plan



# **Phase 1** (current)
- **Barebones Release**: Launch the mod as a “Developer Tool” for server hosts and other modders.
- **Loot Engine**: Biome-dependent rewards.

# **Phase 2:**
- **Basic Almanac**: Implement the [[_The Almanac Book]] that tracks Name, Description, and Habitat tracking of fish caught.
- **Minigames**: Create [[_Minigames]] that can be turned off and on
	-  [SkillCheck](https://x.com/rm20killer/status/2009363725447279041), Stardew Valley style, Fish Fish Revolution, Button Masher

# **Phase 3**
- **The Gear Overhaul**: Introduce the upgradeable Rods, Hooks, Reels, and Lines.
- **Custom loot**: Populate every Zone and specific custom loot and fish

# **Phase 4**
- **Boss Encounters**: Introduce the first Boss Fish - massive, multi-stage battles.
