# What is Angler's Almanac
Angler's Almanac is a fishing mod. It turns a simple pastime into a deep, progressive system involving discovery and rare collections.


--- 
# Reason to use Angler's Almanac

## Fishing
Fishing would be gamified making players having to do a small [[Minigames]] and those minigame scaling depending on the reward tier. With a [[Dynamic Loot Pools]] what you hook depends entirely on where you are. The mod merges **Global**, **Zone**, **Biome**, **[[Bait]]** data to build a custom "deck of cards".

## The Almanac book
The heart of the mod is [[The Almanac Book]], your personal guide to the waters of Orbis:
