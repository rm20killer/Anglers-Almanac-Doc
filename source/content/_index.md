# What is Angler's Almanac
Angler's Almanac is a fishing mod. It turns a simple pastime into a deep, progressive system involving discovery and rare collections.

Here is the [[_Roadmap]] for the mod.

--- 
# Reason to use Angler's Almanac

## Fishing
Fishing would be gamified making players having to do a small [[_Minigames]] and those minigame scaling depending on the reward tier. With a [[Dynamic Loot Pools]] what you hook depends entirely on where you are. The mod merges **Global**, **Zone**, **Regions**, **[[Bait]]** data to build a custom "deck of cards".

## The Almanac book
The heart of the mod is [[_The Almanac Book]], your personal guide to the waters of Orbis:
- **Track Your Progress:** Every fish you catch is recorded with info about the fish.
![[Almanac_Stats_Test.png]]
