# Whisperfrost Frontiers
Fish in Zone 3 would be **Arctic & Sub-Zero Extremophiles** (fish adapted to freezing temperatures, high oxygen levels, and waters that are often trapped under thick layers of ice or glacial runoff).

> [!note] 
> **Arctic Extremophiles** - Aquatic environments where fish survive in temperatures ranging from -2°C to 10°C.
