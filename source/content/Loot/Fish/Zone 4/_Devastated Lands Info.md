# # Devastated Lands
Fish in Zone 4 would be **Volcanic & Ash-Choked Extremophiles** (fish adapted to extreme heat, high acidity, and water clouded by volcanic soot or flowing through obsidian canyons).

> [!note] 
> **Volcanic Extremophiles** - Aquatic environments where fish survive in temperatures ranging from 30°C to 60°C, inhabiting acidic sulfur springs, lava-adjacent pools, and rivers thick with tectonic sediment.
